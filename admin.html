UPI Reconciliation

Upload the GooglePay statement PDF(s) for a period — the "Money Received" export is fine, one per account. Matched payments are saved permanently; unmatched orders stay pending until a later statement clears them or you ignore them. PDFs are read on this device.

Pending — needs a matching credit

Marked UPI in the POS but not matched to a credit yet. Upload the other account's statement to clear them, or ignore ones you know were cash or exceptions.

Extra credits — no order
